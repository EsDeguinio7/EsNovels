Description/Sypnosis

The story of an imperfect, degraded spiritual detective girl and her servant, a young man, solving mysteries related to the dead.

Touka Fujisaki and Saku Fujisaki. Born under a strange fate, and having lost their goals in life, the two seek the meaning of each other’s existence in the madness of the world.
