Heat The Pig Liver

Novel Info's

Status: Ongoing

Genre: Adventure, Comedy, Ecchi, Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, Seinen

Author: Sakai Takuma

Illustrator: Tohsaka Asagi

Volumes: 7

Original Publisher: Dengeki Bunko

English Publisher: N/A

Fan Translation: Hayane

Description/Sypnosis

When I reincarnated, I became a pig! But if I get a cute girl to take care of me, this kind of adventure doesn’t sound bad at all.

I’m an ordinary otaku that lost consciousness after eating raw pig liver. Just when I figured I was reincarnated into another world, I found out I became a pig!

Jess is a girl who can read people’s minds, and she helped me when I was stuck inside a pigpen.

Oink! Cute! From a pig’s perspective, I could catch a glimpse of something underneath her skirt hem, it was white…

“Um, I can hear your thoughts…”

Oh crap! My desires are being exposed!

“If you so desire, just a little bit is okay.”

Eh, what…?!

A life where an innocent girl takes care of me and accepts (albeit a bit taken aback) my beast-like desires. Yeah, it’s not so bad being a pig.

This was supposed to be our oink-oink-like grand adventure, but… Hey Jess, why is your life being targeted?

Now, this pig with no magic or skills must use his knowledge, wits, and sense of smell to save this girl from her cruel fate!
