The Gal Is Sitting Behind Me, And Loves Me

Novel Info's

Status: Ongoing

Genre: Romance, School Life

Author: Michinoku Koharu

Illustrator: Hizuki Higure

Volumes: 2

Original Publisher: Fujimi Fantasia Bunko, Kadokawa

English Publisher: N/A

Fan Translation: I Cant Read JapaneseTL

Description/Sypnosis

No common contact whatsoever, and I got liked by her… and my school life got drastically changed!?

Having been spending my days only studying without even a single friend, I saved her out of just a mere whim.

That’s how it should be, but—

“I could hardly wait for after school! …Hey, kiss me.”

That incident led me to start dating Shino.

Spending time together after school, going along with each other’s hobbies, frequent sleepovers…

She is kind and always sticks to me.

Moreover, my classmates took notice of me, gave me a makeover, and my surroundings also got noisy.

It’s making me more restless than when I was alone, but… well, I guess this kinda school life is also not bad.
