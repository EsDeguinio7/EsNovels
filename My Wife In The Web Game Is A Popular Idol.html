My Wife In The Web Game Is A Popular Idol

Novel Info's

Status: Hiatus

Genre: Comedy, Romance, School Life, Slice of Life

Author: Aborn

Illustrator: Kanda Done

Chapters: 77

Original Publisher: Syosetu

English Publisher: N/A

Fan Translation: pemuda tuna wisata

Description/Sypnosis

Kazuto Ayanokoji is an ordinary high school boy.

He spends his days playing online games as a hobby.

One day, he discovers the true identity of a friend who is so close to him that they are married in-game.

It turns out to be a cool idol from the same class, Rinka Mizuki.

As Rinka tries to act as his wife in real life, Kazuto is forced to lead a dizzying life.
