Description/Sypnosis

Youth–A time that ignites wishes, and sometimes offers entry for the evil. And that night, my youth raised its first cries, together with the flames illuminating the night.

Having forgotten his phone at school, Arihara Aruha sees no other choice but to sneak back inside, when he catches wind of an odd shine lighting up the rooftop. There, he met a girl, dancing beneath the starry sky–Burning.

“If you tell a soul about this…I’ll ruin your life.”

Threatened by the girl, Itou Ioka, Aruha is forced to help her, as he learns more about her, and what lies beneath her perfect facade. The pressure as a model, her rivalry with her colleague, the looming threat of a stalker, and the wish she keeps close to her heart.

“I…should have learned more about her.”

Dreams, wishes, youth…They never turn out perfect. Especially not if a devil takes over and tries to accomplish your wish–by possessing you.
